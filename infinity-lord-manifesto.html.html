The World Law Loop
The Operating System of Civilization

"Safety and order are not goals to be pursued — they are the inescapable output of a correctly designed system."

It functions as the operating system for all physical and digital territories under the Infinity Lord protocol. Just as a computer's operating system manages resources, resolves conflicts, and maintains system integrity without visible effort, the World Law Loop governs civilization beneath the surface — invisibly, continuously, and without failure.
